MatRabatt

In my concept development class we had a task to design a phone app. My app was called MatRabatt and basically it's supposed to find grocery shops with food products on sale. Some features my app had was being able to click on different store brands and seeing what kind of products they had on sale. You could check dfferent food categories like dairy and meat to see what food was on sale and which store brand it was a sale at. It also has a shopping list and the address so you could find where the stores are.
