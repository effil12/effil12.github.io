Photo Documentary

Human Rights Lens Project

In my production class we had a project where we had to choose one of the human rights and make a photo documentary with one of the human rights in mind. I chose "Standard of living" as my human right. My grandma was my model and I took pictures of old photos from when she was young and pictures in present time to show a documentary of her life, from young too old. I named the photo documentary "Livet til ei fra bygda".
